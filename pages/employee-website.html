Employee Management System Website

By Israa Al-Ghaithi
Mar 14, 2019

Designing a website for highly confidential information was not among my expectations this year! It was during my 3-month training period at the university from which I graduated. We were asked to design a special website called Employee Management System. This website helps the HR, PR and others department to Follow and Manage the foreigner college's staffs and students information. The team consists of 3 trainees including me. The funniest thing about this team is that my 2 other colleagues are data management major and I'm the only software developer. Crazy if you think about it. Of course, we used my favorite web language and tools, PHP, Html, CSS, MySQL, Bootstrap and JavaScript. There was some kind of prior agreement between us and our project supervisors, in case we do not complete the project because the training period was short, there is nothing to worry about, it will be completed by new trainees. This is what happened, we only completed like 80% of it but it was super fun to have such experience.
