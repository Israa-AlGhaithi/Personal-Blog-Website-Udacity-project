SMAP Website

By Israa Al-Ghaithi
June 15, 2018

One of the scariest nightmares for students is the final year project. If you overcome this obstacle, embrace yourself right now. Thankfully, I did it and I'm here to brag a little bit about it. My 3 team friends and I used these tools PHP, Html, CSS, MySQL, Bootstrap, JavaScript, Sensors and Arduino to create Smart System for Monitoring Air Pollution. It is a self-service, user-friendly and an intelligent system developed to monitor air quality and enabling citizens every day in Sultanate of Oman to check air quality system. SMAP embeds IoT technology in its infrastructure where various electronic sensors are connected to the internet.

How it works

These connected sensors will measure and collect environmental parameters such as temperature, humidity, etc. and upload these values to a cloud service. In the cloud the data are analyzed and if the retrieved data are above or below a certain threshold limit an SMS is published at the exact moment. Such a new technology will help stakeholders such as the Ministry of Environment and the Ministry of Health to support residents with continuous information about air conditions and for preventive care and fast access to the citizen who have serious problems like asthma, cancer, and heart disease.
